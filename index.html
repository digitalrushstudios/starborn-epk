Artist Bio
Queens, NYC

Built Different.
Born Ready.

Out of South Jamaica, Queens — one of the most unforgiving proving grounds in hip-hop — emerged $tarborn. A name that carries its own thesis: some stars are manufactured, some are lucky. $tarborn is the rare thing in between — a artist who built himself from the ground up and was born with an undeniable gift for the craft.

A proud member of Dot Mobb, $tarborn has carved a lane where the streets meet the studio. As a battle rapper he's proven himself on stages where only the sharpest survive — bars so precise they hit like warrants. As a recording artist, he writes, produces, and engineers his own projects, keeping full creative control from concept to cut.

His catalog spans raw EPs crafted entirely in-house, collaborative projects with the Mobb, and documentary and podcast theme songs that show the reach of his pen. The brand is $$AB — Some Stars Are Built Born — and every release is another bar of proof.
